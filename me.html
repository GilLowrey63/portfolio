About Me

Right now I teach first grade at a small charter school and I love it. the students are a lot of fun and I enjoy learning from my principal and fellow coworkers. When I am not teaching or coding i love hanging out with my wife and our two awesome bulldogs, Groot and Penny. Penny is the skateboarding dog and it is her absolute passion. She has been skating for close to 7 years now and it never stops amazing me. People ask if we are trying to teach Groot to skate, but he prefers to lend himself to a more sedimentary lifestyle and we respect that choice.

When we aren't with our dogs, my wife and I love going to movies and playing different sports. I have been playing hockey since I was 16 and my wife started playing a couple of years ago. It is a lot of fun getting to play with her and she's getting pretty good. My wife has been playing volleyball and basketball since she was in middle school. Since she played hockey, I tried both volleyball and basketball (I grew up in a small Wyoming town with no concrete so I had never played either before), and didn't too horribly. It is safe to say she is a better hockey player than I will ever be at basketball or volleyball.

We enjoy our time together very much and love to dream about the future. We hope to have kids one day and we hope to eventually have a cabin in the woods. We also dream of traveling around not only the country, but hopefully the world. If we can't find the time or means though, we are happy to take our little camper up to the mountains, or make a fort in the living room with our dogs. Either way, life is good and it's easy to smile.
